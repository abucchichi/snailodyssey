About the game

Snails Odyssey is an exploratory NFT game on the WAX blockchain that lets players embark on a slow and steady adventure across a world of gardens and sea.

In Snails Odyssey, players take control of their own snail, equipped with a sturdy shell and a sense of adventure. The world is comprised of lush gardens, dense forests, and rolling hills, all of which are ripe for exploration. As players journey through this world, they'll encounter friendly bugs, hidden treasures, and obstacles that they'll need to overcome.

All players start in the tranquil Garden of Eden, where they can gather resources and gear up for their journey. From there, they can set out on their snail-paced travels, making their way through the gardens and forests, solve puzzles, and encountering all sorts of creatures along the way. Some of these creatures will be friendly, offering help and rewards to the brave snails who venture their way. Others will be less hospitable, and players will need to use their wits and cunning to avoid danger.

After a while this whole beautiful snail world will take its final form and open its doors to all comers.

Sea Snail

Sea Snail are NFTs that can be built in-game or obtained from Snail Treasure. They are used in the game for sea races, expeditions and puzzles. Each Snail has individual random characteristics.

Garden Snail

Garden Snail are NFTs that can be built in-game or obtained from Snail Treasure. They are used in the game for farming, expeditions and puzzles. Garden are the main source of SHELL in the game.

Snail Treasure

Snail Treasure are limited-edition NFTs, which can only be obtained once. Snail Treasure openers receive a withdraw of SHELL without сommission.

Game mechanics

Race

By sending Sea Snail on a race, you can get SHELL tokens, and a chance to get a rare treasure! Choose a territory and start the race!

Exploring

Send your snails to explore world. If your exploring are successful, you'll be rewarded with great rewards! Start your treasure hunt now!

Puzzles

Snails can solve puzzles. There are 3 attempts per day. If you win you can get some tokens.

Battles

Participate in PvP battles on your Snails against sea monsters. Earn SHELL tokens for every victory you achieve.
